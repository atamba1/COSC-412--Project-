About Us

The Maryland Parks Association (MPA) exists to encourage the youth to enjoy the outdoors, while continuing to grow educationally through the use of educational applications. We make the process of finding tickets for your favorite parks simple, creating a one-stop location for all your park needs. Since 2000, we have provided great satisfaction for our users and we look forward to continuing our efforts to educate and inspire the community.
